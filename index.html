About Kupa Warner

Brisbane Videographer & Cinematographer with 8+ Years Industry Experience

I'm Kupa Warner, a Brisbane-based videographer, cinematographer, and director specializing in high-end commercial video production, corporate videography, and cinematic content creation.

With 8+ years of professional experience, I've built a career crafting visually compelling stories for global brands, major events, and ambitious creative projects. My work spans commercial campaigns, social media content, music videos, live events, documentary storytelling, and government productions.

My cinematography has been recognized with a Best Cinematography award at the Vaughan International Film Festival. I've served as cinematographer for Conor McGregor branded content and as Lead Videographer for major Australian events including PAX Australia and SXSW Australia.

From directing 7-person production crews to solo shoots, I bring technical expertise in Sony FX6 cinema cameras, advanced lighting, aerial cinematography with DJI Mavic 3, and professional post-production in DaVinci Resolve and Adobe Creative Suite.

Based in Brisbane, Queensland, I work with clients across Brisbane CBD, Fortitude Valley, South Brisbane, West End, New Farm, Gold Coast, and Sunshine Coast - delivering cinematic video production that elevates brands and tells stories that resonate.

Frequently Asked Questions

Brisbane Video Production FAQ

What areas of Brisbane do you service?

I provide videography services across all Brisbane areas including Brisbane CBD, Fortitude Valley, South Brisbane, West End, New Farm, Paddington, Woolloongabba, and surrounding suburbs. I also regularly work on the Gold Coast and Sunshine Coast for commercial and event video production projects.

What types of video production do you specialize in?

I specialize in commercial video production, corporate videography, event coverage, music videos, and social media content creation. My experience spans TVCs, brand films, training videos, conference coverage, documentary-style productions, and platform-optimized content for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and LinkedIn.

What equipment do you use for video production?

I shoot on professional cinema-grade equipment including the Sony FX6 cinema camera, Sony A7III, DJI Mavic 3 for aerial cinematography, professional lighting setups, gimbals, Steadicam systems, and multi-camera broadcast configurations. Post-production is handled in DaVinci Resolve, Adobe Premiere Pro, and After Effects for color-graded, broadcast-quality delivery.

How much does a videographer cost in Brisbane?

Video production costs vary based on project scope, duration, and complexity. Contact me for a custom quote tailored to your specific needs. I offer competitive rates for Brisbane businesses while maintaining cinema-grade production quality and award-winning creative direction.

Do you work with large production crews?

Yes, I regularly lead multi-person production crews and can scale teams based on project requirements. From solo shoots for quick-turnaround content to full-scale productions requiring multiple camera operators, lighting technicians, and sound engineers, I adapt to your production needs.

How long does video production take?

Timelines depend on project complexity. Social content can turnaround in 24-48 hours. Commercial projects typically take 1-3 weeks from shoot to delivery. Complex productions with multiple locations or heavy post-production may take 4-6 weeks. I provide detailed timelines during consultation to meet your deadlines.

Can you handle both filming and editing?

Absolutely. I provide end-to-end video production services including concept development, filming, post-production, color grading, sound design, and final delivery. This ensures creative consistency throughout the project and streamlined communication for Brisbane clients.
